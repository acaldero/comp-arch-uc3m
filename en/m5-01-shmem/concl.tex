\section{Conclusion}

\begin{frame}[t]{Summary}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textemph{Multiprocessor} as computer with \textmark{multiple highly coupled processors} with
        coordination, use, and memory sharing.

  \mode<presentation>{\vfill\pause}
  \item \textemph{Multiprocessors} classified into 
        \textmark{SMP} (Symmetric multiprocessors) and
        \textmark{DSM} (Distributed Shared Memory).

  \mode<presentation>{\vfill\pause}
  \item Two \textmark{aspects} to consider in memory hierarchy: 
        \textgood{coherence} and \textgood{consistency}.

  \mode<presentation>{\vfill\pause}
  \item Two alternatives in \textmark{cache coherence}: 
        \textgood{directory} and \textgood{snooping}.

  \mode<presentation>{\vfill\pause}
  \item \textemph{Snooping protocols} \textbad{do not require} a \textgood{centralized element}.
    \begin{itemize}
      \item But they generate more bus traffic.
    \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
